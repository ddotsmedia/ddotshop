# DdotsShop Roadmap

Phases 0–24 are built. Planned next:

- **Phase 25 — AI Personalization Engine**: customer segmentation, re-engagement & win-back campaigns.
- **Phase 26 — Multi-vendor Marketplace Mode** (Agency plan): multiple sellers under one storefront.
- **Phase 27 — Click-to-WhatsApp Ad Manager (CTWA)**: ad creation + attribution.
- **Phase 28 — Catalog Sync**: Shopify/WooCommerce product sync + CSV import.
- **Phase 29 — AI Live Agent Handoff**: shared inbox, escalation, knowledge base.
- **Phase 30 — B2B Wholesale Mode**: MOQ, tiered pricing, trade accounts.
- **Phase 31 — AR Product Viewer**: WebXR, 3D model upload, beauty try-on overlay.
- **Phase 32 — UAE Delivery Integration**: Aramex, DHL, Fetchr, Noon Express.
- **Phase 33 — White-label Agency Reseller Portal**.
